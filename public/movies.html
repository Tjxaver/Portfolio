My Favorite Movies

The Movies that Made Me

  1. The Princess Bride
    • "As you wish."
    • One of the wittiest, most quotable movies ever made; complete with fencing, fighting, torture, revenge, giants, monsters, chases, escapes, TRUE LOVE, and miracles!

  2. The Matrix
    • "I know kung-fu."
    • A deeply philosophical movie disguised as an action flick that revolutionized the field of visual effects.

  3. LotR: The Fellowship of the Ring
    • "You! Shall! Not! Pass!"
    • Fantastic filmography in beautiful locales bring to life one of the richest fantasy epics ever told.

  4. Dark City
    • "Sleep..."
    • A mind-bending delve into the nature of human identity and the power of human will.

  5. The Boondock Saints
    • "En Nomine Patris, et Fili, et Spiritus Sancti."
    • A gritty tale of vigilante justice that explores the moral quagmire created when faith and familial loyalty lead good men to violently confront the corrupt, criminal element in their neighborhood.
